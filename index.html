Categories

Animes

Shonen

Manga aimed at teenage boys are called shōnen. They are series with large doses of action, in which there are often humorous situations. The companionship between members of a group or a combat team is emphasized.

Romcom (Romance Comedy)

Romantic comedy (also known as romcom or rom-com) is a subgenre of comedy and romance fiction, focusing on lighthearted, humorous plot lines centered on romantic ideas, such as how true love is able to surmount most obstacles.

Isekai

A typical theme of light novels, manga and anime that revolves around a character or a group of characters from Earth who intentionally or accidentally end up in a parallel universe, a fantasy world, on another planet, where they must adapt to a different world, or a different world, on another planet, where they must adapt to a different world.

Videogames

Platform

sometimes referred to simply as platformers, is a genre of video games where the player is in control of a character who must advance through a stage by walking, running, jumping, bouncing or climbing over a series of platforms and cliffs, defeating enemies.

Shooter

Shooting video games or shooters are a genre that encompasses a large number of subgenres that have the common feature of allowing you to control a character that, as a general rule, has a weapon (mostly firearms) that can be fired at will.

Survival horror

Survival horror is a subgenre of horror and action-adventure video games that focuses on the character and their journey for survival, while at the same time trying to scare the players with different elements.
